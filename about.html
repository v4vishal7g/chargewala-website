Our Story

Building India's Smart EV Charging Network

ChargeWala started with one simple idea — finding an EV charging station should be as easy as finding a fuel station.

We are building a platform that connects EV drivers with trusted charging stations while helping station owners grow their business. Our goal is to make electric mobility more convenient, reliable and accessible across India.

As India's EV ecosystem grows, ChargeWala aims to become the bridge between drivers, charging partners and modern technology.
