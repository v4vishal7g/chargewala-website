1. Introduction

Welcome to ChargeWala. ChargeWala is an EV Charging Platform that helps electric vehicle owners discover verified charging stations, navigate to charging locations, submit reviews, and connect with charging partners. By accessing our website or mobile application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency regarding how your data is collected, used and safeguarded.

Acceptance Of This Policy

By accessing or using ChargeWala's website, mobile application or services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood and agreed to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please discontinue using our services.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a secure and reliable EV charging experience, ChargeWala may collect different types of information when you use our website or mobile application.

Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • User Profile Information
  • Profile Photo (if uploaded)

Location Information

With your permission, ChargeWala collects your device location to help you discover nearby charging stations, calculate distances, and provide navigation support through Google Maps.

Booking Information

  • Charging Station Bookings
  • Booking Status
  • Booking History
  • Charging Date & Time

Reviews & Ratings

When you submit a review, we store your rating, review message, booking reference and the charging station associated with your review.

Partner Information

Charging station owners who register as ChargeWala Partners may provide business details, station information, contact details and related documents during the onboarding process.

3. How We Use Your Information

ChargeWala uses the collected information to provide, improve and secure our services.

  • Create and manage your ChargeWala account.
  • Show nearby EV charging stations.
  • Process charging bookings.
  • Display verified customer reviews.
  • Improve charging station recommendations.
  • Provide customer support.
  • Respond to enquiries submitted through the Contact Form.
  • Send important service notifications.
  • Detect fraud and improve platform security.
  • Improve app performance and user experience.

ChargeWala uses trusted third-party services to deliver its platform.

  • Firebase Authentication for secure login.
  • Cloud Firestore for storing application data.

These services may collect technical information as governed by their own privacy policies.
