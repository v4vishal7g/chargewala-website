Account Deletion Process

How to Delete Your ChargeWala Account

You can permanently delete your ChargeWala account directly from the mobile application without contacting support.

Open App

Open the ChargeWala mobile application and sign in using your registered mobile number.

Go to Profile

Navigate to your Profile screen from the bottom navigation bar.

Delete Account

Tap the Delete Account option and carefully review the confirmation message.

Confirm

Confirm your request. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted.

What Happens After Deletion?

Your Personal Data Is Permanently Removed.

Once your account deletion request is confirmed through the ChargeWala app, your account and associated personal information are permanently removed from our active systems, except where retention is required by applicable law.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions You May Have

Can I recover my deleted account?

No. Once your account deletion request is completed, it cannot be reversed. You can create a new account later using your mobile number if you wish to use ChargeWala again.

How do I delete my account?

Simply open the ChargeWala mobile app, go to Profile, tap Delete Account, and confirm your request.

Do I need to contact support?

No. The account deletion option is available directly inside the ChargeWala app. Contact support only if you experience any issues.
