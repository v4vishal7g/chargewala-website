Delete Your ChargeWala Account

At ChargeWala, we respect your privacy and give you complete control over your personal information. This page explains how you can permanently delete your ChargeWala account and the data associated with it.

Delete Process Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

ChargeWala is committed to protecting your personal information. You can permanently delete your account directly from the ChargeWala mobile application at any time.

How to Delete Your ChargeWala Account

You can permanently delete your ChargeWala account directly from the mobile application without contacting support.

Step 1

Open the ChargeWala mobile application and sign in using your registered account.

Step 2

Go to Profile from the bottom navigation menu.

Step 3

Tap on the Delete Account option available in your profile settings.

Step 4

Confirm your request. Your account and associated data will then be permanently deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you choose to delete your account, ChargeWala permanently removes your personal data from our systems, except where retention is required by applicable law.

Personal Information

  • Profile Information
  • Registered Phone Number
  • Profile Photo
  • User Preferences

Activity Data

  • Bookings
  • Favorites
  • Notifications
  • Reviews & Ratings
  • Chat History

Partner Data

  • Charging Stations
  • Earnings Records
  • Wallet Information
  • Withdrawal Records

Data Retention Policy

Once your account deletion request is confirmed through the ChargeWala app, your account and associated personal information are permanently removed from our active systems.

Certain information may be retained only if required by applicable laws, regulatory obligations, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or legal enforcement. Any retained data will be handled securely and only for the legally required period.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my ChargeWala account?

Open the ChargeWala app, go to Profile, tap Delete Account, and confirm your request.

Will my data be permanently deleted?

Yes. Your account and associated personal data are permanently removed from our active systems after your deletion request is processed, except where retention is required by applicable law.

Can I recover my deleted account?

No. Once the deletion process is completed, the account cannot be restored. You can create a new account using your mobile number if you wish to use ChargeWala again.
